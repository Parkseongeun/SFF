Unsolicited Idea Submission Policy
Samsung Electronics and its employees do not accept, review or
consider ideas, suggestions, concepts and the like that have not been
officially requested by us (“Unsolicited Idea Submissions”).
This is to prevent any potential dispute or misunderstanding as to the
similarity between Unsolicited Submissions and our internally and
independently developed products, technology and services, and further to
advise potential submitters to take care to protect your own creative
endeavors.
We ask for your understanding of the purpose of our policy and that you
do not submit any unsolicited general ideas or suggestions in conceptual
stages.
